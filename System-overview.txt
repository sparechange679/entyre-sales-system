Sales and Inventory Management System - Summary
What It Is
A web-based financial and inventory management system designed specifically for Entyre Limited, a tire distribution and automotive service company in Malawi with branches in Blantyre, Lilongwe, Salima, and Nkhotakota.
The Problem It Solves
Entyre Limited currently operates entirely manually using Excel spreadsheets, handwritten invoices, and phone calls. This creates:

Frequent data entry errors and duplications
Delayed and inaccurate financial reports
Poor cash flow visibility
Difficulty tracking inventory levels
Time-consuming manual reconciliation
No real-time business insights

What The System Does
Core Functions:

Financial Management

Automatically records all sales transactions in real-time
Generates professional invoices with company branding
Creates quotations for customer requests
Tracks payments (cash, bank transfers, mobile money)
Produces financial reports (income statements, balance sheets, daily sales)


Inventory Control

Tracks tire and spare parts stock levels in real-time
Sends automatic alerts when stock runs low
Records stock movements (sales, receipts, adjustments)
Maintains product catalogs with specifications and pricing


Customer & Sales Management

Maintains customer records (contact info, service history)
Processes sales transactions
Manages service appointments and breakdown requests
Supports both individual and corporate clients


Payment Integration

Integrates with Mpamba and Airtel Money (local mobile payment platforms)
Processes multiple payment methods
Maintains complete payment audit trails



Technical Overview

Platform: Web-based (accessible via browser on any device)
Technology: HTML/CSS/JavaScript frontend, PHP backend, MySQL database
Architecture: N-tier (separates presentation, business logic, and data layers)
Access: Role-based permissions for managers, accountants, technicians, and customers

Key Benefits

Eliminates manual data entry and associated errors
Provides real-time financial visibility for better decision-making
Automates invoice and quotation generation
Enables mobile access for field technicians
Reduces financial reporting time from days to minutes
Improves inventory control and prevents stockouts
Supports business growth across multiple branches

In essence, it's transforming a paper-based tire and automotive service business into a digitally-managed operation with accurate, real-time financial and inventory control.
